1. Test that MMA is alive for a Natural Language Understanding installation using the following commands:
  echo "Port forward to model-management-api service on port 4001"
  kubectl port-forward svc/{{ .Release.Name }}-ibm-watson-mma-model-management-api 4001:4001 --namespace {{ .Release.Namespace }}

curl -k -X GET 'https://localhost:4001/natural-language-understanding/api/v2/health'
